Mission

Mama’s Caribbean Grill is a restaurant founded to serve Authentic Jamaican Food prepared with society’s health and nutrition in mind. Our employees are valued and are held to standards of excellence when preparing and serving our cutomers.

Atmosphere in The Workplace

Mama’s Caribbean thrives when there is a healthy work environment. All employees will have respect for each other, communicate with cordiality and understand the concept of hard work paying off. We want our employees to WANT to come to work.

History Behind the Craft

Reid’s Caribbean Grill opened in the year 2000 in Charlotte, NC by Vinroy Reid. In 2001, Vinroy’s mother, Hazelyn Mills, visited Charlotte and he convinced her to stay and cook for his new location while he returned to the construction industry. Out of his love for his mother and admiration and respect for his mother’s hard work he named the new restaurant Mama’s Caribbean Grill. The Charlotte location opened in 2003 and is still a thriving business today. The restaurant continued to grow as a second location openined in 2007 in Brooklyn, New York as a partnership between Vinroy and his brother, Donald Allen. Vinroy has endeavored to share his mother’s cooking with those in Charlotte and Atlanta.
